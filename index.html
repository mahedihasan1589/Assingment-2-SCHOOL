Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Children aged 3–6 years fall into the early
childhood education stage, often referred
to as kindergarten or preschool.

Elementary School

typically serves children aged 6 to 12 years and focuses on building
foundational academic, social, and emotional skills. It prepares students for higher education

Middle (10-16) Years

serves as a transitional stage between elementary and
high school. This phase focuses on deeper academic learning and social development.
